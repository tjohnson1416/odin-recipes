Cheesburger Recipe

Description

The cheesburger is the perfect vessel. Every since high school, the cheeseburger has been my go-to meal. While making a good cheeseburger isn't super hard, making a bad cheeseburger is incredibly easy. Recently, tons of burger joints have been opening up in my area, and with them has come a slew of burgers that can be best described as pretentious. Too often the problem with these burgers is an over-emphasis on the toppings while neglecting the the star of the show, the patty. My hope is to guide people through the process of making a humble, but delicious burger. No arugula, swiss cheese, or (heaven forbid) caviar. Just a plain, tried and true American cheeseburger. Enjoy.

Ingredients

Makes 4 burgers

  • 4 - Hamburger buns
  • 1 1/3 lbs - Ground beef 80/20
  • 4 slices - American Cheese (Yes, you read that right)
  • 1 - White onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups - Lettuce, shredded
  • 1 - Large tomato, sliced
  • Pickles (If you're gross)
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Condiments as desired

Instructions

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins

  1. Preheat griddle to medium high heat
  2. Lightly oil griddle and start to grill onions. Be sure to season them with salt to taste.
  3. Remove onions from griddle after they're soft and put aside for later.
  4. Separate ground beef into 1/6 lb balls. Should have 8 balls.
  5. Increase the heat of griddle to high.
  6. Place balls of ground beef on the griddle and use press to flatten them
  7. Salt and pepper the tops of the patties to taste. This is the only time you'll season the meat, so make sure you use enough.
  8. After 3-5 mins prepare to flip the patties by using the previosuly grilled onions to make four small piles of onions on the griddle.
  9. Flip four of the eight patties on top of the onions. Flip the other four on the griddle normally.
  10. Immediately top each patty with American cheese
  11. When the cheese is melted, place the onion patties on top of the regular patties
  12. Transfer each set of patties to a toasted bun and place toppings and condiment as desired
  13. Enjoy and thank me later
